Samsung Smart TVs Are on Sale So You Can Upgrade Your Home Theater

You can now save hundreds thanks to Walmart’s deals on over a dozen models.

By Paige Szmodis
Aug 13, 2019
Samsung

It may already be easy to get sucked into a binge-worthy TV show, but 4k smart TVs make it even easier. Samsung has a wide range of smart TVs with Ultra HD, LED, or QLED features from 43 to 82 inches in size—and right now a bunch of them are on sale at Walmart.

What's the Difference Between OLED and QLED TVs?

All of these televisions have smart functionality so you can connect to your preferred streaming service, whether its Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hulu, HBO NOW, VUDU, CBS, Google Play, or YouTube. With four times the resolution of Full HD, built-in Wi-Fi, and at least one HDMI and USB port, these models have top image quality and seamless connectivity. Samsung also provides a PurColor system for wider color spectrum, a SmartThings app to control and monitor the TV, a Stream Link app to stream games, and a Universal Guide that combines content from all of your apps so you can spend more time watching and less time scrolling.

There’s a model for every size and budget here. Go for a top-of-the-line QLED TV if you want the best quality for low-light shots, pick the curved TV for enhanced depth, or select one of the Frame models if you want your TV to blend in with your wall art.
